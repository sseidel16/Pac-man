Ms Pac-man
Created By: Stephen Seidel
Version: 1.5

---Directions---
press:
the up and down arrow keys during any menu to navigate
the arrow keys during gameplay to navigate
enter during any menu to select
the space bar during gameplay to pause or unpause
m during gameplay to minimize and pause
escape during any animation to skip animation
escape during gameplay to enter save menu
escape during any menu to exit instantly
s during load menu to exit and mute or unmute sound

---Notes---
game names can only have letters and numbers
press arrow keys just before Ms Pac-man reaches an intersection for best results

---Cheat Codes---
press:
f to release fruit
h to get highscore
n to go to the next level
p to eat a pill
r to exit cheat mode
